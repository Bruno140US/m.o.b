About Me

Music Artist and Composer

Growing up to heavy metal brands such as Metallica, Pink Floyd, Korn and Limp Biskit, made me fall in love with different heavy crowd suffering sounds then mixed it up with some 2Pac, Lil Wayne, T.I and Jeezy, I grew up the passion to record music. Latin family and raised in Miami, I want to spread my music all over the world.
